Paris Belle-Mort

Paris Belle-Mort comes off as an easy going monarch with great leadership skills and no problems, but inside his brain, there lives a mess. I mean for him to be a representation of both princes and princesses in fictional works, so he often waits for someone to save him (that someone being Bee) when he is perfectly capable and able to save himself. He often gets annoyed at repetitive actions and flawed logic, so he hates, hates, HATES Ren, the most selfish being on Earth. On the contrary, he enjoys hanging out with the clowns, especially Deine (and Paris also continuously tries to recruit Deine as the student council treasurer).
