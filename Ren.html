Ren Dumms

Ren Dumms is a mystical being. She may look like an ordinary girl, but she has a secret: She can swallow multiple people by merely inhaling. This ability is very useful when it comes to annoying people she doesn’t want to deal with. As the embodiment of all shojo manga protags, Ren often gets the favour of the background characters, so long as she’s not around someone with a higher social ranking than her. But even then, because she is a master-class manipulator, she is always able to flip things for her favour. Take Paris, the president of the school, who absolutely hates her. Why can’t he stand her? Because she takes every moment possible to annoy him. Another great example is Bee except Ren generally does want to be her friend and is not able to turn her annoying switch off.
